Devotion

Philippians 4:13: I can do all things through Christ who strengthens me.

This verse teaches that God can help us overcome challenges and give us courage in difficult times.

Ecclesiastes 3:1: To everything there is a season, and a time to every purpose under the heaven.

This verse provides comfort and patience, especially during difficult times, as it reassures us that God has a plan and timing for everything.

1 John 4:19: We love Him because He first loved us.

God's love is the foundation and reason for our love. Our ability to love others flows from the fact that God loved us first.
